About Project Med & Ed

Our Mission & Overview

Project Med & Ed is a youth-led initiative and service project based in Wake County, North Carolina, dedicated to improving health access and education in underserved communities. We provide and distribute first aid kits, hygiene kits, mental health kits, and essential medical supplies, while also offering health education programs, events, and blogs. Through partnerships with local nonprofits and the support of community members, we strive to empower underprivileged and under-resourced populations with the tools and knowledge they need to live healthier, safer lives.

What We Provide

Our Core Services

  • First Aid Kits: Distribution of essential kits to promote immediate safety and response.
  • Hygiene Kits: Providing necessary personal care items for health and dignity.
  • Essential Medical Supplies: Delivering needed supplies to under-resourced populations.
  • Health Education Programs: Workshops and resources focused on preventative care and well-being.

Our Area of Impact

Project Med & Ed is proudly based in Wake County, North Carolina, where we focus our efforts to address local needs. However, our vision is to extend our support and services across all of North Carolina, partnering with organizations statewide to maximize our positive impact.
